About Lauren Powers

Lauren was born November 20, 1986 in North Babylon, NY to Monica & Harold. Her mother, Monica Powers is a Children's Librarian and her father, Harold Powers just retired from the NYS Unified Court System as a Court Officer. She has a sister, Vanessa who is a very talented artist- check out her work here.

Lauren attended the State Univeristy of New York College at Purchase and graduated with a B.A. in Liberal Studies in 2008. Currently she lives in Astoria, Queens and works in retail financial services.
